Batman, often referred to as the Dark Knight, is one of the most iconic and enduring superheroes in the world of comic books and popular culture. Created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger, Batman made his first appearance in Detective Comics #27 in 1939. Unlike many other superheroes, Batman possesses no superhuman abilities; instead, he relies on his exceptional intellect, physical prowess, and an array of advanced gadgets to fight crime in Gotham City. His alter ego, billionaire Bruce Wayne, uses his wealth and resources to fund his vigilantism, seeking justice and vengeance for the murder of his parents when he was a child. With his menacing bat-inspired costume and unwavering commitment to his crusade, Batman has become a symbol of fear to criminals and a beacon of hope to the citizens of Gotham. His complex character, dark past, and enduring dedication to fighting injustice have made him a beloved and timeless figure in the world of comics and beyond.
